ABOUT US

CNVCert - Your Trusted Partner

CNVCert (Chung Nhan Viet) was established with the mission of providing management system and product certification services to international standards, contributing to enhancing the competitiveness of Vietnamese businesses.

As a certification body designated by the Ministry of Science and Technology, CNVCert operates according to ISO/IEC 17021 (management system certification) and ISO/IEC 17065 (product certification) standards, ensuring objectivity, transparency, and professionalism in all certification activities.

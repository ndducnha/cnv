FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQ - Your Questions Answered

Common questions about CNVCert ISO certification services

How much does ISO certification cost?

Certification costs depend on business scale (number of employees, number of facilities), type of ISO standard and certification scope. Please contact hotline 0369 777 713 for detailed and free quotation.

How long does it take to get ISO certification?

Typically 2-4 months depending on the business's readiness and type of standard applied. With CNVCert's optimized process, the timeline can be shortened if the business already has a basic management system.

How long is ISO certificate valid?

ISO certificate is valid for 3 years provided that the business maintains the system and passes annual surveillance audits. CNVCert will accompany the business throughout this process.

Is CNVCert certificate internationally recognized?

Yes, CNVCert is designated by the Ministry of Science and Technology, operating according to ISO/IEC 17021 and ISO/IEC 17065 standards. Certificates issued by CNVCert are valid and recognized globally.

What should businesses prepare before audit?

Businesses need to prepare documentation system, management procedures according to the corresponding ISO standard, and ensure the system has been operating for at least 3 months. CNVCert consulting team will provide detailed guidance and support throughout the preparation process.

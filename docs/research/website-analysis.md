# PSI Việt Nam Website Analysis

## Company Type
**Certification Body** - PSI (Phòng Thử Nghiệm Pin Lithium) is a designated testing and certification laboratory specializing in electrical-electronic product testing, battery testing, and compliance certification under ISO/IEC 17025 standards.

---

## Homepage Structure & Sections

### Header
- Fixed navigation bar with PSI logo
- Language toggle (VN | EN)
- Primary navigation menu (see Navigation Structure below)

### Hero Section
- Tagline: "TOP 1 PHÒNG THỬ NGHIỆM PIN LITHIUM" (Top 1 Lithium Battery Testing Laboratory)
- Image carousel featuring:
  - Battery testing services
  - Camera cybersecurity
  - Telecommunications equipment
  - SAR testing
  - Tracking devices
  - Certification consulting

### Core Content Sections

**1. About/Company Introduction**
- Emphasizes expertise in electrical-electronic product testing
- Claims designation by Ministry of Science and Technology
- Operating under ISO/IEC 17025 standards

**2. Why Choose PSI** (Value Propositions)
- Expert consulting team
- Fast response and dedicated advice
- Professional methodology
- Long-term partnership focus

**3. Certifications & Recognition**
- Carousel displaying accreditation certificates and designations
- Visual emphasis on credibility and compliance

**4. Services Grid**
- Eight service cards with icons
- Focus on QCVN (Vietnamese standards) compliance
- International certification offerings

**5. Partners Section**
- Logo carousel of major clients
- Demonstrates market presence and trust

**6. News & Updates**
- Regulatory information carousel (VBPL)
- Featured articles and industry news

### Footer
- Background: Blue (#69A6C5)
- Company logo and full name
- Contact: Address, phone, website, email
- Social: Facebook link
- Maps integration (location embedding)
- Recognition/credential statement

## Visual Design Elements

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: White background
- **Accent**: Blue (#69A6C5) - used in footer, buttons, highlights
- **Text**: Dark gray for body content
- **Neutral**: Gray for secondary elements

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Montserrat (throughout entire site)
- Consistent sizing hierarchy for headings and body text

### Spacing & Layout
- Responsive padding for desktop/mobile optimization
- Grid-based service card layout
- Carousel sliders for dynamic content display

### Interactive Features
- Multi-level dropdown menus
- Image carousels (hero, certifications, partners, news)
- Form inputs in footer/contact sections
- Maps iframe for location
